NFPA Zabaan is an early prototype machine translation tool aimed at automatically translating the technical language used in NFPA code and standards. NFPA Zabaan is built on leading open source neural machine translation and natural language processing platforms and is being developed by data scientists in NFPAâ€™s Research and Data Analytics department. Right now, Zabaan focuses on English to Spanish translations and we hope to expand to other languages in the future.

Although Zabaan is still very much a work in progress, here are a few situations where we hope Zabaan may be of use in the future:

  • NFPA subject matter experts who need quick, ad hoc translations of code-related content
  • Professional translators working with NFPA text that contains very domain-specific language
  • Officials who need to engage with non-English speaking communities on technical fire and electrical safety concepts
  • Public education teams and researchers who want to efficiently translate entire files such as reports and fact sheets.
